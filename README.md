# ThanatoGenesis - RimWorld Mod

ThanatoGenesis is a RimWorld mod that allows you to craft mech serums and a new, less exotic serum capable of reviving pawns, albeit with possible permanent aftereffects.

---

## 🧪 Overview

This mod introduces a set of high-risk, high-reward biotech systems inspired by archotech technology and extreme genetic engineering.

Features include:
- Crafting Healer Mech Serums
- Crafting Resurrector Mech Serums
- Crafting Luciferium
- A new resurrection serum that is cheaper but may leave permanent aftereffects

---

## ⚙️ Features

---

## 📦 Installation

1. Download the mod.
2. Place the folder into your RimWorld `Mods/` directory.
3. Enable **ThanatoGenesis** in the mod list.

---

## 🧩 Compatibility

- Should be compatible with most mods unless they modify the game's resurrection mechanics.

---

## ⚠️ Notes

- Luciferium crafting is disabled by default, even after completing its research project. My reasoning is that the crafting of Healer and Resurrector Mech Serums can be justified through the use of Luciferium, but nobody truly knows how Luciferium itself is manufactured. Because of that, I believe its recipe should remain unavailable by default.

  However, I know some players may want to craft Luciferium anyway, so I included an option to enable it. If you simply want a consistent way to revive pawns, I recommend using the Thanato-Genesis Serum instead. It does not require Luciferium, but it may leave the revived pawn with permanent health conditions, which can later be treated with a Healer Mech Serum or other mods.

- If you're curious about the lore behind the Thanato-Genesis Serum: unlike Luciferium, it does not rely on mechanites to repair damaged tissue. Instead, it massively accelerates the body's natural healing processes. This approach is far less precise than mechanite-based reconstruction, which can result in permanent health complications for the resurrected pawn.
